Jalapeno Burger

Ultra-Hot, Home-made Jalapeno Burger

As a second to our threesome comes the Jalapeno-Burger for the real Gurmans

Burger more hot then your mom.

Ingridients

  • 50g of seeded and chopped Jalapeno pepper
  • 50g of cream cheese, softened
  • 250g ground beef
  • 100g burger buns, split
  • 20g of mayo
  • 35g of Sriranchia
  • 5g of salt and pepper

How to

  1. Preheat a grill for medium heat, when hot lightly oil the grade
  2. Stir together jalapenos and cream cheese
  3. Divide ground beef into 2 portions
  4. Spoon the cream cheese mixt into the center of upper pattie
  5. Put Sriranchia with mayo on the lower pattie
  6. Salt and pepper the beef
  7. Cook the beef until its well done, about 10 minute per side.
