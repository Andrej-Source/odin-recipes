BBQ Hot-Dog

Classic Drazdiak BBQ-Hot-Dog

This classic home-made BBQ-Hot-Dog, inspired by timeless

classic made by Drazdiak best personal

Ingridients

  • 2 Pikok sousages
  • 1 Lidl Hot-Dog bun
  • 20g of Lidl Grill BBQ
  • 2 slices of beacon
  • 10g of chopped onion
  • 1 piece of chopped garlic

How to

  1. Bring pot of water to boil, then reduce heat to low
  2. Put the pikok sousages inside and cook for 5 minutes
  3. Place steamer basket into the pot with the Bun for 2 minutes
  4. Place BBQ, 2 slices of beacon, onion and garlic inside the bun
  5. Finish the dish with both sousages, enjoy!
