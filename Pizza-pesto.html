Pizza Pesto

Delicious Home-made pizza

Home-made pizza recipe, perfect for busy students,

with special taste and tons of healthy fats.

Ingridients

  • Lidl pizza crust
  • 200g of smotana
  • 200g of Pesto
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 150g of chicken breast, chopped
  • 300g of Mozzarella cheese
  • 5g of Oregano
  • 3 pieces of garlic, chopped

How to

  1. Preheat the oven to 230 C
  2. Preecook the chicken breast on bit of Sunflower oil
  3. Put the Pizza crust on the sheet, with safety paper
  4. Spread the smotana eavenly on the crust
  5. Put Mcheese on Top
  6. Spread the tomato, garlic and chicken on Top
  7. Bake in the Preheated Oven until cheese is melted
  8. Spread pesto evenly on the pizza
  9. Finish with the 5g of Oregano
